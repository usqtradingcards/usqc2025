Frequently Asked Questions
Why Imgur?
Your photo may not load correctly. And your downloaded trading card may appear all black.
A more technical answer: Imgur typically allows cross-origin resource sharing, which is required for this website.
How should I upload to Imgur?
Upload to Imgur here. After upload, right-click on the photo > copy image address.
Why doesn't the photo appear?
If you used Dropbox or Imgur, right-click on the photo and copy image address.
A more technical answer: Dropbox & Imgur blocks CORS but Dropbox and Imgur's CDN does not.
Why is the black border so thick?
For printing purposes! The bleed zone ensures important text & images are not cropped. The finished card will be cut at the trim line.
